This document is a structured reference file for testing the Spoorthi Chatbot assistant.

Important note:
- This is mock and demo-friendly context for development.
- It represents Spoorthi Fest as conducted by the JNTUH ECE Department.

## Event Overview

- Event name: Spoorthi
- Event type: National-level technical and cultural symposium
- Organizing department: Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering, JNTUH
- Meaning of Spoorthi: Inspiration
- Purpose: To host technical competitions, workshops, project showcases, presentations, and cultural activities for students

## Institutional Context

### About JNTUH

JNTUH College of Engineering, Hyderabad is a premier engineering institution known for strong academics, practical exposure, and professional training.

### About the ECE Department

- Department: Electronics and Communication Engineering
- Established: 1973
- Reputation: One of the most preferred departments in Telangana
- Academic reach: 1000+ students across undergraduate and postgraduate programs

## Spoorthi Identity

- Spoorthi is the flagship symposium of the JNTUH ECE Department.
- It has been conducted for over 22 years.
- It combines technical events and cultural activities.
- It attracts participation from multiple engineering colleges.
