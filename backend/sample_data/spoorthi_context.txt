SPOORTHI FEST 2026 - COMPLETE COMPREHENSIVE REPORT

1. Institutional Overview & Event Identity
- Spoorthi Fest is the annual techno-cultural symposium conducted by the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) at Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Hyderabad (JNTUH).
- The name "Spoorthi" means inspiration and reflects the goal of encouraging innovation, creativity, and collaboration among students.
- The ECE department at JNTUH is known for academic excellence, technical exposure, and a strong student community.
- Spoorthi began around 2004 as a technical fest and later expanded into a broader techno-cultural event around 2009.
- The fest brings together students from different colleges to learn, compete, collaborate, and enjoy a mix of technical and cultural experiences.

2. History & Evolution
- Spoorthi has grown steadily in scale and popularity over the years.
- Earlier editions included paper presentations, project expos, quizzes, workshops, and cultural activities.
- Later editions introduced hackathons, industry collaborations, guest sessions, and online competitions.
- Editions such as Spoorthi 2013 and Spoorthi 2014 were held over two days in March and attracted students from engineering colleges across the state.
- Spoorthi is widely regarded as a flagship student-facing event of the JNTUH ECE Department.

3. Organization & Management
- Spoorthi is organized by the ECE Department of JNTUH at its main engineering college campus.
- The fest is conducted through the combined efforts of faculty members and student teams.
- Students actively take responsibility for planning, coordination, execution, finance, and on-ground management.
- The collaborative model gives students practical exposure to teamwork, leadership, communication, and event management.

14. Legacy & Social Impact
- Spoorthi has a legacy of more than 20 years.
- The fest combines technical learning, creativity, teamwork, and student engagement.
- It has also included socially meaningful initiatives such as food donation drives and road safety awareness activities.
- Spoorthi continues to inspire students to explore both technical and creative talents.
