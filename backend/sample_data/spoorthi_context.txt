SPOORTHI FEST 2026 – COMPLETE COMPREHENSIVE REPORT
1. Institutional Overview & Event Identity
Spoorthi Fest is the annual techno-cultural symposium conducted by the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) at Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Hyderabad (JNTUH).
The term 'Spoorthi' means inspiration, reflecting the goal of encouraging innovation, creativity, and collaboration among students.
The ECE department, established in 1973, supports over 1000 students and is known for academic excellence and technical exposure.
Started in 2004 as a technical fest, Spoorthi evolved into a techno-cultural event in 2009 and continues as a flagship event.

2. History & Evolution
Spoorthi has grown significantly over the years with increasing participation and event diversity.
Earlier editions included paper presentations, project expos, quizzes, workshops, and cultural activities.
Recent years introduced hackathons, industry collaborations, and online events.

3. Organization & Management
The fest is organized by faculty and students collaboratively, giving students real-world experience in leadership and event management.
